Events

Kota Tinggi, located in the state of Johor, Malaysia, offers a variety of events and activities that cater to different interests. Here are some examples of events and activities that people can enjoy in Kota Tinggi:

Nature and Adventure Activities

Kota Tinggi Waterfall Visit:

This popular natural attraction is perfect for picnics, swimming, and enjoying the scenic beauty of the waterfall.

Kota Tinggi Waterfall

Firefly Watching Tour:

Take a boat ride along the Johor River at night to witness the magical sight of fireflies illuminating the mangrove trees.

Firefly Watching Tour

Hiking and Camping at Gunung Panti:

Hiking up Gunung Panti offers a challenging yet rewarding experience with breathtaking views.

Gunung Panti

Cultural, Agricultural and Eco-Tourism

Kota Tinggi Historical Museum

Learn about the rich history and culture of the area through various exhibits and artifacts.

Kota Tinggi Historical Museum

Desaru Fruit Farm

Take a guided tour of this extensive fruit farm, enjoy fruit tasting, and learn about various tropical fruits and farming practices.

Desaru Fruit Farm
